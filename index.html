Controlling the Covid Crisis

This website is for the purpose of educating you on the current Covid-19 Crisis, and provide resources for you to help those in need. During this crisis, small businesses as well us the underprivilaged populations are suffering, and we must do all that we can to help them.

Check out the Essential Information tab for general information about the virus. The Donations tab will give you an insight into organizations that aim to help parts of the population during these uncertain times, and the Small Businesses tab will feature an interview with a local small business, as well as other businesses that you can support.
