Learn more about the creators of this website: Mannat Dhot, Sudiksha Vaidyanathan, and Palina Volskaya

About The Creators


Mannat, Palina, and Sudiksha are a group of high schoolers from Dublin, California. They were led to create this site because of the huge impact that COVID-19 has had on people's lives and livelihoods. They are active members of their school's Speech and Debate Team. And all of them have a passion for helping the community and trying to do so in the middle of this crisis.


About CCC


The background of our idea was based on the individual brainstorm each of us had done prior to meeting as a group. All of us had ideas related to helping control the spread of COVID-19, each in our different ways. This made us realize that COVID-19 was a very interesting and pressing issue- and is something many are curious about. Our final product is a website that offers a variety of resources to help those suffering from the economic impact of the Covid-19 Crisis. The goal of our website was to provide quick, easy, and simple information to our viewers.


About Our Website


Our Essential Information page shares eye catching statistics that shows the drastic growth and spread of the virus.The Donations page offers a link to a list of charities on the front line and explains what each charity is doing. It also focuses on 5 main charities and describes the key information about each.The Small Businesses page features virtual interviews for 2 Bay Area Based Businesses and additional hyperlinks, videos, and images for 6 other Bay Area-based businesses to help give viewers an insight on how they can help these small businesses during these unprecedented times.
